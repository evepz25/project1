Sneakers

I wanted to make this page dedicated to my slight obsession with shoes. I have been an advide collector since late 2016 up until now. I have always liked sneakers since i was little and I actually thought it was a phase. That was a lie! I mostly collect Nike's and Jordan's. I have tried to get into different brands but Nike/Jordan seem to have me in a chokehold. Right now I can say i have about 55 pairs of shoes and unfortunately still counting. That may seem like a lot but to me it is a small collection compared to others!
